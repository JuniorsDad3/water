Water Conservation Tips
  • Shorter Showers: Save up to 20 liters per minute.
  • Fix Leaks: A dripping tap wastes about 15 liters per day.
  • Efficient Appliances: Use water-efficient fixtures to cut down usage.
  • Water Your Garden Wisely: Water during cooler parts of the day.
